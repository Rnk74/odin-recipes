Mashed Potatoes

Description :

Everybody seems to have their favorite way of making mashed potatoes. Some cook them with the peel on, some without. Some add a little of the cooking water to the mashed potatoes for extra starch. I have found that the single thing that makes the biggest difference for making perfect creamy, heavenly potatoes is the type of potatoes you use.

Ingredients :

  • 1 1/2 pounds (680 g) Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cut lengthwise into quarters
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 tablespoons (60 ml) heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons (30 g) butter
  • 1 tablespoon milk (or more)
  • Salt and pepper

How to Make Mashed Potatoes Step-By-Step :

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade

  1. Cook the potatoes
  2. Prep the butter and cream
  3. Drain and mash the potatoes
